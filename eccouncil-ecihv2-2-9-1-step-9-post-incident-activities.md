What is the process flow for Post-Incident Activities? -

These activities help to evaluate and improve the effectiveness of the incident
response processes.

Post-incident activities will help the responders to assess lags in security
posture, settings, and configurations across the organization.

	▪ Incident Documentation
	▪ Incident Impact Assessment
	▪ Review & Revise Policies
	▪ Close the Investigation
	▪ Incident Disclosure
